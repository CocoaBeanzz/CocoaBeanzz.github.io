<div class="col">
Eidolon: Fate of Fools is a mystical arena shooter inspired by Major Arcana tarot cards. Indulge your fate by collecting cards within the arenas and by looting defeated foes. Dominate the lobby by cleverly utilizing both the Upright and Inverted abilities of each card. Upright abilities often resemble traditional arena shooter weapons, while Inverted abilities can offer unique movement tech or provide a tactical edge: at the cost of discarding that card. Master these arcane powers as you fight to prove your divinity and achieve apotheosis.
