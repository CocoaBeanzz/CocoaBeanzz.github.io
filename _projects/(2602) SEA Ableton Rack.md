<div class="col" markdown=1>
I encoded the entire alphabet into spectrogram-decodable audio. 

Letters begin at C2 and progress upwards chromatically. 

Frequency: Shifts the frequency of the characters, cleanly translating them vertically on a spectrogram.
Velocity: Velocity sensitivity of the rack.

This is a pretty barebones proof of concept that I'd love to expand into a full VST! Let me know what features you'd like to see from it.
