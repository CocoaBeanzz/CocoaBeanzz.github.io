"Cats & Critters: A Dungeon Claw-er" is an enchanting party game that casts up to four players as cat wizards working together to defeat the evil dragon Frank and recover the golden yarn. This whimsical, pick-up-and-play experience captures the essence of miniature tabletop adventures with its simple controls and low-poly art style. Grab a few friends to help you defeat pesky critters, solve some puzzles, and earn magical loot. Along the way, use a variety of spells and collect the most sparkles to prove that you’re the coolest cat!
